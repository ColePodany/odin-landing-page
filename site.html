All About Sharks

Sharks are fascinating creatures that inhabit our oceans. They are apex predators and play a crucial role in maintaining the balance of marine ecosystems.

A Sampling of Sharks

Bull shark: a powerful predator found in coastal waters.

Hammerhead shark: has a distinctive head shape.

Leopard shark: a smaller species found in coastal waters.

Whale shark: the largest fish in the world.
